## 🔹 Version 3 (Final)

### Prompt
Generate a structured shift handover summary from the following notes. Do NOT use paragraph format.

Highlight only critical information:
- Behaviour incidents or escalation  
- Medication administered, refused, or missed  
- PRN usage  
- Incontinence or personal care concerns  
- Participant mood and engagement  
- Any risks or alerts for next shift  

Use bullet points.
