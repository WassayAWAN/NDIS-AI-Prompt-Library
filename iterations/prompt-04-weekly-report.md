## 🔹 Version 3 (Final)

### Prompt
You are a house supervisor preparing a weekly participant report. Based on the following case notes, generate a structured report including:

- Summary of Week  
- Number and Nature of Behaviour Incidents  
- Changes in Behaviour (increase/decrease)  
- Activities and Community Access  
- Positive Progress and Achievements  
- Health Updates and Medical Appointments  
- Safety Concerns or Hazards  
- New Observations (not previously reported)  
- Recommendations and Support Adjustments
